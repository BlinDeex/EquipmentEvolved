Equipment Evolved is a comprehensive overhaul of Terraria's equipment progression, replacing the standard flat-stat vanilla modifiers with a deep, ARPG-inspired loot and augmentation system.

Your equipment is no longer just a collection of numbers—it dictates your playstyle. Modifiers now fundamentally alter how your weapons, armor, and tools function.

⚙️ Core Changes
Vanilla Prefixes Disabled: By default, vanilla prefixes are disabled to prevent pool dilution (configurable). Existing items with vanilla prefixes will retain them.

Stat Squish: To accommodate the high power level of these new mechanics, global weapon damage and armor defense have been reduced

Earlier Access: The Goblin Tinkerer is now available as soon as you acquire your first reforged weapon. (Note: He will not sell items until his vanilla requirements are met).

Auto-Reforge Recommended: using an auto-reforge mod alongside this one is highly recommended. It allows you to see exactly which custom reforges are available for your item and displays their exact stats, making the experience much smoother.

Infinite Scaling: The mod will be equally useful in the early game and Calamity endgame because everything scales dynamically off of your own base stats!

⚒️ The Reforging Overhaul
The Goblin Tinkerer has learned a few new tricks. You can now reforge almost every piece of equipment in the game to gain entirely new mechanics:

Weapon Reforges: Transform your weapons with massive mechanical shifts. Turn a melee weapon into a mana-consuming powerhouse, or make your ranged weapons shoot ricocheting bullets.

Armor Reforges: Armor pieces can now be rolled! Universal reforges can be applied to any armor piece (e.g., Phalanx, which grants projectile reflection), while Specialized reforges are exclusive to Headwear, Chestplates, or Leggings (e.g., rolling Dashing on your chestplate, or Desperate on your leggings).

Tool Reforges: Pickaxes can roll traits that reveal ores or mine entire veins at once, while Axes can roll fortune modifiers to increase wood drops.

🧿 The Charms System
A brand new, RNG-based loot layer. Enemies and bosses drop equippable Charms of varying rarities. Applying Charms to your gear grants unique stats ranging from bonus damage to increased invincibility frames or lifesteal.

How to use: Hold the Charm and Right-Click an item in your inventory to equip it. Applied Charms cannot be recovered, only replaced.

Shapes: Charms drop as a Circle (Weapons), Square (Armor), or Triangle (Accessories).

Tiers: Common > Rare > Epic > Legendary > Mythical.

Augmentations: High-tier Charms may possess augmentations that specifically buff a certain Reforge type, changing its behavior in some way (like the Clearing augmentation expanding your mining radius).
    
The Exalted Tier: Rumors say there is a charm tier above Mythical, and it is the only one capable of stabilizing the Omni augmentation... if you are lucky


🧩 Deep Synergies & Builds
Every reforge interacts with the rest of your equipment in various ways, allowing countless unexpected and powerful synergies to emerge, wont be showing examples of builds, just an example reforge from most categories:

[Melee] Untouchable:
Damage ramps up infinitely as long as you do not take damage. At higher ramp-up levels, swing speed begins increasing as well. If you are hit, you lose the accumulated bonus and take multiplied incoming damage based on your current ramp-up level.

[Ranged] Flow State:
Applies a stacking bleed to enemies on hit. The bleed deals a percentage of the weapon's base damage, mitigated by the enemy's defense. If you fail to maintain consistent hits, the stacks decay rapidly.

[Magic] Triple Shot:
Each projectile spawns additional projectiles with altered trajectories. Increases mana cost and decreases damage.

[Whips] Detonating:
Whipping your own minions rigs them with explosives. The bomb detonates on their next enemy contact, dealing massive burst damage scaling with minions damage

[Axe] Fortune:
Chance to drop additional items when cutting trees.

[Universal armor] Chrono:
Piece Bonus: Increased movement speed.
Set Bonus (Active): Time Stop. Freezes enemies and projectiles in a radius around you. Frozen objects do not deal contact damage.


[Universal armor] Phantom:
Piece Bonus: Increased true damage from all sources
Set Bonus (Active): Summons two clones that mimic your actions. Reactivate the ability to make them charge and explode. While active, you are inflicted with these status effects:
Voidseal: Stops all health/mana regeneration and prevents potion usage
Soulburn: Causes hallucinations and damage over time that escalates the longer it lasts
This armor set has an augmentation: [Phantom augmentation]: Increases phantoms count, you become immune to voidseal effects but soulburn ramping speed increases


[Specialized armor - Leggings] Quantum:
Converts all dashes into an instantaneous, omnidirectional teleport that can bypass solid obstacles. Teleporting leaves behind a high-energy afterimage that rapidly collapses to your new location, dealing damage to any enemies caught in its path scaling with defense.
