Equipment Evolved is a comprehensive overhaul of Terraria's equipment progression, replacing the standard flat-stat vanilla modifiers with a deep, ARPG-inspired loot and augmentation system.

Your equipment is no longer just a collection of numbers—it dictates your playstyle. Modifiers now fundamentally alter how your weapons, armor, and tools function.

⚙️ Core Changes
Vanilla Prefixes Disabled: By default, vanilla prefixes are disabled to prevent pool dilution (configurable). Existing items with vanilla prefixes will retain them.

Stat Squish: To accommodate the high power level of these new mechanics, global weapon damage and armor defense have been squished

Earlier Access: The Goblin Tinkerer is now available as soon as you acquire your first reforged weapon. (Note: He will not sell items until his vanilla requirements are met).

Auto-Reforge Recommended: using an auto-reforge mod alongside this one is highly recommended. It allows you to see exactly which custom reforges are available for your item and displays their exact stats, making the experience much smoother.

Infinite Scaling: The mod will be equally useful in the early game and Calamity endgame because everything scales dynamically off of your own base stats!

⚒️ The Reforging Overhaul
The Goblin Tinkerer has learned a few new tricks. You can now reforge almost every piece of equipment in the game to gain entirely new mechanics:

Weapon Reforges: Transform your weapons with massive mechanical shifts. Turn a melee weapon into a mana-consuming powerhouse, or make your ranged weapons shoot ricocheting bullets.

Armor Reforges: Armor pieces can now be rolled! Universal reforges can be applied to any armor piece (e.g., Phalanx, which grants projectile reflection), while Specialized reforges are exclusive to Headwear, Chestplates, or Leggings (e.g., rolling Dashing on your chestplate, or Desperate on your leggings).

Tool Reforges: Pickaxes can roll traits that reveal ores or mine entire veins at once, while Axes can roll fortune modifiers to increase wood drops.

🧿 The Charms System
A brand new, RNG-based loot layer. Enemies and bosses drop equippable Charms of varying rarities. Applying Charms to your gear grants unique stats ranging from bonus damage to increased invincibility frames or lifesteal.

How to use: Hold the Charm and Right-Click an item in your inventory to equip it. Applied Charms cannot be recovered, only replaced.

Shapes: Charms drop as a Circle (Weapons), Square (Armor), or Triangle (Accessories).

Tiers: Common > Rare > Epic > Legendary > Mythical.

Pity System: You are guaranteed Legendary and Mythical drops after a certain number of dry rolls. Use the /checkpity command to check your current status.

Augmentations: High-tier Charms may possess augmentations that specifically buff a certain Reforge type, changing its behavior in some way (like the Clearing augmentation expanding your mining radius).

The Exalted Tier: Rumors say there is a charm tier above Mythical, and it is the only one capable of stabilizing the Omni augmentation if you are lucky...

🧩 Deep Synergies & Builds
Every reforge interacts with the rest of your equipment in various ways, allowing countless unexpected and powerful synergies to emerge. Here are couple examples of what you can build:

The "Immortal Blood Mage" Build

Weapon: A Vampiric ranged weapon. This reforge grants flat lifesteal on-hit, though it does decrease your firing speed.

Armor: The complete Cursed universal armor set. Each individual piece provides a bonus of increased lifesteal. When all three pieces are equipped, the passive set bonus allows you to ignore all incoming damage that falls below a specific threshold of your maximum health. However, any damage that breaks this threshold will instantly deal a massive percentage of your maximum health, regardless of the attack's actual raw damage.

Accessories: A combination of Blood Forged and Hollow accessories. Blood Forged increases your damage while decreasing your maximum health. Hollow decreases your total health even further, but compensates by increasing all of your mobility stats.

How it works: The goal of this build is to intentionally shrink your maximum health pool to be as small as possible using the Blood Forged and Hollow accessory reforges. 
Because the Cursed armor set punishes heavy, threshold-breaking strikes by dealing damage based entirely on a percentage of your maximum health, having a tiny health pool means the 
actual numerical damage you take is incredibly low. You then leverage the flat lifesteal from your Vampiric weapon and the innate lifesteal piece bonuses from the Cursed armor to instantly 
heal that small amount of missing health back to full before the enemy can land another attack, on the other side of spectrum you can try to get so much defense and hp that enemies rarely hit
you for more than minimum damage threshold of cursed armor resulting in damage being negated altogether


The "Momentum Striker" Build

Weapon: A Kinetic melee weapon. This reforge actively boosts your damage dealt depending on how fast you were moving at the exact moment of the hit.

Armor: The complete Chrono universal armor set. Each individual piece provides a bonus of increased movement speed. When all three pieces are equipped together, 
the active set bonus grants you a Time Stop ability that freezes enemies and projectiles in a radius around you. Frozen objects do not deal contact damage.

Accessories: A full set of Aerodynamic accessories. This reforge increases your movement speed and flight time.

How it works: The goal of this build is to stack as much passive movement speed as possible using the Aerodynamic accessories and Chrono 
armor piece bonuses to constantly maximize the damage multiplier of your Kinetic weapon. Chrono armor set bonus is time stop, it will completely freeze everything within range
for a few seconds, if you want to maximize speed you would use desperate leggings which give tons of extra movement speed when losing health, and since majority of reduced hp sources
in the mod use a custom stat "max health cap" it will reduce maximum point to which you can heal yourself, instead of reducing max health itself, because of this you can effectively
always gain low hp bonuses from other reforges assuming you reduce your health cap low enough

Some reforges are extremely good on their own and other equipment can be utility based, for example delayed ranger reforge makes all your bullets freeze when shot, after a delay
all of them instantly travel along their trajectory passing blocks leaving a laser beam behind damaging everything caught in path, it is designed to kill worm type enemies. 
On the other side of spectrum we got flow state ranger reforge, it applies infinitely stacking bleeding debuff on enemy as long as you maintain it, each stack adds a percentage of weapon 
damage to the bleed, as you can guess it is extremely good against immobile and big singular bosses with lots of hp.
