⚔️ Overview
This mod aims to completely overhaul the Terraria reforge system, replacing vanilla prefixes with game-changing mechanics that become a crucial aspect of your build.

Core Changes:

Vanilla Prefixes Disabled: By default, vanilla prefixes are disabled to prevent pool dilution (configurable). Note: Existing items with vanilla prefixes will retain them.

Stat Squish: To accommodate the high power level of these new reforges, global weapon damage is reduced by 30% and armor defense by 25%. This encourages players to engage with the reforge system immediately.

Earlier Access: The Goblin Tinkerer is now available as soon as you acquire your first reforged weapon.

[WIP] He will not sell items until vanilla requirements are met.

[IDEA] All items will drop pre-reforged with random new prefixes.

Recommendation: I highly recommend using the "Auto Reforge" mod alongside this one. It allows you to see exactly which custom reforges are available for your item and displays their exact stats, making it much easier to cook up specific builds.

🛡️ Armor Reforges
There are two distinct categories of armor reforges:

1. Universal Reforges
These exist for all armor pieces. They are individually weaker but grant a Set Bonus when all three pieces share the reforge.

Mechanic: Set bonuses can be Passive or Active (keybind). Activating a set bonus applies a global cooldown to all armor abilities. You cannot "hot swap" armor during combat to cycle abilities.

Universal Types:

Phalanx:

Piece Bonus: Increased Damage.

Set Bonus (Passive) - Reactive Plating: If a projectile dealing substantial damage (or lethal damage) is about to hit you, the armor shoots a piece of itself to intercept it, creating an explosion. Long Cooldown.

Symbiotic [IDEA]:

Piece Bonus: Increases stats gained from Charms (Charms can only be applied once). Equipment cannot be removed without destroying it.

Set Bonus (Passive): Increases applied Charm level by +1. If a new Charm is Legendary or higher, an Augmentation is forcibly applied.

Augmented:

Piece Bonus: Grants 1-3 random buffs at specific defense thresholds.

Set Bonus (Passive): % Damage increase for every active buff on the player.

Chrono:

Piece Bonus: Increased movement speed.

Set Bonus (Active): Time Stop. Freezes enemies and projectiles in a radius. Player becomes intangible. [Balance Note: May need windup time].

Phantom [WIP]:

Set Bonus (Active): Summons two clones that mimic your actions. Reactivate to make them charge and explode.

Cost: While active, you are inflicted with these debuffs:
Voidseal - Completely stops all health and mana regeneration and prevents respective potions usage.
Soulburn - Causes hallucinations and damage over time. Effects grow stronger the longer it lasts.

Cursed:

Piece Bonus: +1 Lifesteal.

Set Bonus (Passive): Ignore all damage that is less than 5% of your Max HP. However, any damage above 5% instantly deals 80% Max HP damage regardless of the actual amount.

🧿 Charm System
Charms are obtained by killing enemies (varying chances) and bosses (10x chance).

How to use: Hold the Charm and Right-Click an item to equip it.

Tiers: Common (2%) > Rare (1%) > Epic (0.5%) > Legendary (0.1%) > Mythical (0.02%) > Exalted (0%).

Pity System: Guaranteed Legendary every 30 drops; Guaranteed Mythical every 100 drops.

Shapes: Circle (Weapon), Square (Armor), Triangle (Accessory). Shapes are random.

Restriction: Applied Charms cannot be recovered, only replaced.

Augmentations [IDEA]:
Legendary+ Charms may have an "Augmentation" that specifically buffs a certain Reforge (e.g., Phantom Augmentation reduces the Soulburn penalty of Phantom Armor).

🧠 Strategy Guide: Synergies & "Downgrades"
At first glance, some reforges in this mod may look like "downgrades" or detrimental to your character. However, these mechanics are designed for niche, high-level theory-crafting.
 By combining specific negative traits with the right synergies, you can break the game's standard limitations.

The "Cursed" Armor:
The Cursed set bonus forces you to take damage equal to 80% of your Max HP whenever a hit exceeds 5% of your health. Normally, this means you will die in exactly two hits, regardless of your defense.
However, if you are fighting a boss while severely undergeared, this reforge bypasses your lack of defense and effectively doubles your survivability. This set can also serve for drain tank playstyle by
combining high lifesteal and health reducing reforges on accessories which would help you heal back to 80% of hp faster, combine all of this with additional iframes from charms or accessories and window
where you can die will get very narrow.


"Untouchable" Weapons:
The Untouchable reforge continuously scales your damage up for as long as you avoid taking damage. The catch is that getting hit multiplies the damage you receive based on your ramp-up level,
 meaning a heavy blow could easily one-shot you. While beating a boss flawlessly without taking a single hit is incredibly difficult, you can mitigate this risk heavily in multiplayer.
  By combining an Untouchable weapon with the "Silent" leggings reforge to reduce your aggro, enemies are highly likely to ignore you, allowing you to safely stack massive damage from the backlines.

"Inverted" Magic:
The Inverted reforge applies a lethal damage-over-time effect to your character with prolonged use. However, if you are wielding a mana-hungry, high-DPS weapon,
this turns the fight into a thrilling DPS race. Not only does this reforge completely prevent the Vanilla "Mana Sickness" debuff, it actually inverts it—causing your
damage output to increase instead. With the right setup, you can burn down the boss's health bar faster than the reforge burns down yours.
  
"Equalizer" Weapons:
The Equalizer reforge converts your damage into True Damage equal to a set percentage of the target's maximum health. Because of this unique scaling,
an Equalizer weapon will kill an early-game boss and a post-Moon Lord boss in the exact same amount of time! For this reforge, raw damage is meaningless—fire rate is everything.
Finding the fastest-firing gun available is crucial to maximizing your DPS. You can amplify this even further by using the Phantom armor's active ability, which can effectively triple
your damage output for as long as you can sustain the penalties. Combine this setup with True Damage amplifiers from Charms and accessory reforges, and you will effortlessly shred
through bosses with massive health pools.

⚠️ Early Access & Balance Note
This is the first release of the mod, so you may encounter some balancing issues and boring reforges might be later replaced. If you find a mechanic or combination that feels
completely broken, please let me know!

A Note on Modded Weapons: Please keep in mind that applying these custom reforges to modded weapons 
will inevitably result in some completely broken, god-tier synergies. I will not be keeping track of all these cross-mod interactions,
nor will I attempt to balance this mod around them. Balancing around the most overpowered modded weapons would force me to make the baseline reforges
incredibly weak, which would ruin the fun for standard playthroughs. If you find a combination that completely breaks the game—enjoy it!
