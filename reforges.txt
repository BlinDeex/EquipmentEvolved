🗡️ Melee Reforges
Arcane Infused: Requires mana to swing and inflicts Mana Sickness, but massively boosts raw damage. Scales directly with Magic damage bonuses.

Gigantic: Massively increases weapon size at the cost of swing speed.

Perceptive: Boosts Critical Strike chance. The critical damage multiplier scales infinitely with your total crit chance, up to a hard cap.

Titan Force: Heavily increases both damage and knockback.

Ultra Light: Grants auto-swing and drastically increases swing speed, sacrificing raw damage and weapon size.

Untouchable: Damage ramps up infinitely as long as you avoid taking hits. High ramp-up levels also accelerate swing speed. Taking damage shatters the bonus and inflicts multiplied incoming damage based on your lost stacks.

Sealed: Rolls entirely random, highly exotic stats unavailable on other reforges. Applying a Charm beforehand boosts the roll quality based on the Charm's tier. Stats remain hidden until you close the menu. Cannot be reforged again.

Payback: Dealing melee damage knocks coins loose from your inventory. These coins linger briefly before homing in on the struck enemy, dealing damage based on their monetary value. Most coins are recovered on hit.

Kinetic: Multiplies damage dealt based on your exact movement speed at the moment of impact.

Sundering: Reduces base damage, but every strike permanently shreds the enemy's defense.

Gravitic: Swings emit gravitational waves that drag enemies towards you. The pull radius and strength scales inversely with attack speed.

🏹 Ranged Reforges
Adaptable: Accepts any ammo type and significantly boosts fire rate. (Disabled for Rocket Launchers).

Infernal: Weapon damage aggressively scales up as you fight until it "burns out" and disables itself. Near burnout, projectiles trigger violent secondary explosions that scorch nearby enemies.

Challenger: Triggers a high-stakes minigame requiring you to shoot specific colored orbs. Weapon stats are massively multiplied based on your final minigame score.

Controlled: Converts firing mechanics to a high-speed burst fire. (Incompatible with native custom-fire weapons).

Equalizer: Converts physical damage into True Damage based on a percentage of the target's maximum health. Completely ignores defense and damage reduction.

Vampiric: Grants flat, guaranteed lifesteal on every hit at the cost of fire rate.

Delayed: Fired projectiles freeze dead in mid-air. Ceasing fire or reaching the time limit causes all suspended projectiles to instantly blink along their trajectories, leaving devastating, tile-piercing damage trails.

Ricochet: Projectiles violently fracture and split upon striking terrain.

Thaumic: Grants a random positive buff on hit. Prioritizes unapplied buffs to rapidly build a massive arsenal of effects. Hitting with an active buff extends its duration.

Flow State: Inflicts a stacking bleed on hit that deals a percentage of the weapon's base damage (mitigated by defense). Stacks decay rapidly if you fail to maintain relentless fire.

🔮 Magic Reforges
Chaotic: Periodically rerolls a completely new set of stats. Can grant massive, game-breaking damage multipliers, or inflict severe penalties like draining coins, damaging you, or healing enemies.

Endless: Completely removes mana cost, but reduces overall damage.

Inverted: Replaces Mana Sickness with Mana Surge. Mana Surge massively boosts damage but constantly drains your health. Dying triggers a catastrophic explosion that obliterates everything nearby, including other players.

Tidal: Damage scales directly with your current mana pool. Casting while at maximum mana injects the projectile with a massive damage surge.

Splintering: Hits have a chance to violently erupt into random mana shards. Critical strikes guarantee more shards. Shards seek out secondary targets.

Triple Shot: Splits every cast into three projectiles with erratic trajectories. Heavily increases mana cost and reduces base damage.

Overloaded: Holding attack suppresses fire and rapidly drains mana to charge the spell. Releasing unleashes a single, massively scaled, hyper-condensed projectile.

🦇 Summoner Reforges
Frenzied (Minion): Minions move and attack with blistering speed, but suffer a penalty to raw damage.

Monarch (Minion): Resummoning an active minion will not spawn a duplicate. Instead, it absorbs the cast to permanently multiply its size and damage. Stacks infinitely.

Proxy (Minion): Minions freeze in place and become temporary turrets. They perfectly mirror all projectiles you fire. Damage scales from both weapon and minion stats. (Incompatible with worms/upgradeable minions).

Sacrificial (Whip): Striking enemies grants a protective ward. Taking lethal damage instantly executes all active minions to prevent your death, granting invulnerability time based on the number of minions sacrificed. Prevents summoning while on cooldown.

Detonating (Whip): Whipping your own minions rigs them with explosives. The bomb detonates on their next enemy contact, dealing massive burst damage scaled to the minion's base strength.

💍 Accessory Reforges
Aerodynamic: Boosts movement speed and maximum flight time.

Blood Forged: Heavily increases damage but sacrifices maximum health.

Earth Shaper: Drastically increases mining speed.

Efficient: Reduces overall mana usage.

Equilibrium: Grants a balanced boost to defense, minion slots, movement speed, flight time, and health regeneration.

Fortified: Massively increases raw defense.

Lethal: Amplifies critical strike damage.

Revitalizing: Accelerates passive life regeneration.

Risky: Spikes your damage output but cripples your defense.

Sharpshooter: Increases critical strike chance.

Stoic: Trades damage output for heavily increased defense.

Warlord: Increases maximum minion capacity.

Void Piercer: Amplifies all True Damage dealt.

Hollow: Shreds your maximum health, but heavily boosts all mobility stats.

Lucky: Increases Charm drop luck.

Zealous: Boosts raw attack speed.

Greedy: Causes enemies to drop significantly more coins.

⛏️ Tool Reforges
Fortune (Axe): Grants a chance to yield extra loot when chopping trees.

Revealing (Pickaxe): Mining has a chance to briefly pulse and reveal valuable tiles hidden in the darkness.

Vein Miner (Pickaxe): Instantly obliterates entire ore veins at once (limit scales with Pickaxe Power).

Clearing (Pickaxe, Hammer): Destroys tiles in a massive area of effect. Mined tiles have a high chance to turn to dust and yield no items.

Geological (Pickaxe): Mining basic dirt or stone carries a chance to unearth random gems.

🛡️ Armor Reforges
Note: Activating any Universal Set Bonus applies a global cooldown to all armor abilities, preventing hot-swapping during combat.

Universal Reforges
Phalanx

Piece: Increases overall damage.

Set (Passive): Reactive Plating. Incoming lethal or heavy-damage projectiles trigger the armor to eject a physical plate, intercepting and destroying the threat in a localized explosion.

Symbiotic

Piece: Amplifies stats gained from applied Charms. Charms become permanent; the armor cannot be unequipped normally and must be destroyed to be removed (Shift-click).

Set (Passive): Elevates all applied Charms by one full tier. Retains the original rolls but scales their power to the new tier, and generates one entirely new roll. Consumes any existing augmentations to permanently hyper-boost the stats.

Augmented

Piece: Grants powerful positive buffs upon reaching specific defense thresholds.

Set (Passive): Multiplies your damage output for every active buff currently on your character.

Chrono

Piece: Increases movement speed.

Set (Active): Time Stop. Completely freezes all enemies and projectiles in a massive radius around you. Frozen entities cannot deal contact damage.

Phantom

Set (Active): Spawns two shadowy clones that perfectly mirror your actions. Reactivating the ability commands them to charge and detonate. Activation inflicts Voidseal (halts all regeneration and disables potions) and Soulburn (triggers escalating damage-over-time and hallucinations).

Cursed

Piece: Increases lifesteal effectiveness.

Set (Passive): Completely ignores all incoming damage that falls below a specific threshold of your max health. However, any attack that breaks this threshold instantly nukes a massive percentage of your max health, ignoring the attack's actual damage value.

Conduit

Piece: Increases maximum mana capacity.

Set (Passive): Disables normal mana regeneration, replacing it with a rapid, uninterrupted, and infinite flow of mana.

Raid Boss

Piece: Increases the base defense of the specific armor piece.

Set (Passive): Cripples your mobility, but massively boosts total defense, damage reduction, and enemy aggro. You become the wall.

Gladiator

Piece: Increases melee swing speed.

Set (Active): Summons a localized arena ring. Inside the ring, you take reduced damage and gain massive lifesteal and damage amplification. Leaving the ring while an enemy is trapped inside heavily penalizes your mobility and damage. Enemies attempting to flee the ring suffer a burst of damage and severe defense shred.

Overdrive

Piece: Amplifies critical strike damage.

Set (Active): Halts all mana recovery and incinerates your remaining mana pool to fuel a catastrophic burst of using speed and fire rate. The overload violently ends and triggers a cooldown the moment you run dry.

Paradoxical

Piece: Accelerates mana regeneration.

Set (Passive): Completely inverts your health and mana pools. Incoming damage drains mana—if it hits zero, you die. Magic weapons consume health to cast. Grants immunity to Potion Sickness, but Mana Sickness now severely penalizes mana potions.

Eclipse

Piece: Increases maximum health.

Set (Active): Equinox Swap. Instantly shift between Sun, Moon, or Neutral stances. Sun Stance constantly drains health but grants devastating damage and attack speed. Moon Stance drops your damage output but heavily fortifies defense and regeneration. Swapping emits a violent shockwave scaling with your held weapon. Stances only activate while actively using an item.

Specialized Reforges (No Set Bonuses)
Headwear:

Enraging: Maximizes enemy aggro range and targeting priority.

Berserker: Damage output aggressively scales up as your health drops.

Reinforced: Heavily bolsters the base defense of this helmet.

Weak Link: Strips the defense from this helmet, but transfers a massive defense boost to your equipped chestplate and leggings.

Catalyst: Magic attacks inflict Pierced Veil. Striking an afflicted enemy with a Ranged weapon detonates the veil, causing the initial projectile to violently shatter inside them, dealing massive internal damage and continuing its trajectory.

Malignant: Healing potions completely bypass Potion Sickness, but permanently inflict a stacking reduction to your maximum health every time they are used.

Psionic: Massively extends item pickup range and amplifies the healing/mana restored from dropped hearts and stars.

Phasing: All dashes and teleports gain extra invincibility frames, allowing you to slip through attacks.

Chestplate:

Vitalis: Amplifies all healing received from potions and lifesteal effects.

Void: Massively scales all sources of True Damage.

Unyielding: Grants escalating damage reduction as your health pool depletes.

Rebounding: Taking heavy knockback causes the armor to violently eject piercing shrapnel. Getting hit grants a stacking, temporary defense boost. Accumulating too many stacks overheats the armor, causing it to shatter and strip all your defense for a short duration.

Dashing: Grants charge-based dashes that instantly snap you toward your cursor. Does not provide invulnerability.

Conductor: Melee attacks inflict Kinetic Charge. Striking an afflicted enemy with Magic damage detonates the charge for massive burst damage.

Leggings:

Silent: Plummets your aggro, making enemies practically ignore your existence.

Terra: Grants a massive native defense boost to these leggings, but only while your feet are planted firmly on the ground.

Desperate: Movement speed accelerates as you take damage, culminating in a massive mobility spike when hovering near death.

Resonant: Globally amplifies the stats provided by all your accessory reforges.

Weaver: Ranged attacks inflict Arcane Resonance. Striking an afflicted enemy with Melee damage violently detonates it, triggering a localized cluster explosion that scales with your melee damage.

Quantum: Upgrades all standard dashes into instant, omnidirectional teleports that bypass solid matter. Teleporting leaves a volatile energy afterimage that collapses to your new location, shredding any enemies caught in its path with damage scaled from your defense.
